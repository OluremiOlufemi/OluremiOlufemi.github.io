The State of Women's Reproductive Health in 2016

Data and Statistics Provided by the Guttmacher Institute and The Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation

Given the current political climate, women’s healthcare and specifically access to abortion, has been an extremely polarizing issue. In regards to public policy, even with the passing of Roe v. Wade, many states still have very few abortion providers. With the potential threat of a conservative supreme court overturning Roe v. Wade, the following research looks at current access to abortion clinics, the impacts of policy, and women’s reproductive health.
